# About Us
MegaPunk Games is a small Canadian video game solutions and consulting company founded by 20+ years industry veterans with experience in AA and AAA gaming. MegaPunk builds on the knowledge gathered from working at studios like Ubisoft, Krafton, Microsoft and other smaller ones in order to provide industry leading software focused on the latest technology. Our expertise spans diverse disciplines including gameplay, VR, AI, physics, system/engine programming and optimization. Strong from its experience, MegaPunk aims at providing performant and configurable, yet easy to use, software for Unreal Engine and Unity.

# Consulting and help
We know that integrating third party solutions can be a burden. Therefore, we always try to develop our products with an API that makes using and extending the feature set easy. Nevertheless, using up valuable resources to learn and integrate our software can be time consuming and expensive. That is why we offer integration services to get you up to speed as quickly as possible. Whether you are looking for personalized training or an extra hand, be sure to <a href="mailto:contact@megapunkgames.com?subject=Consulting request">give us a shout</a> and see how we can help you.
